Chicken

Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved dish in Indian cuisine. It consists of marinated pieces of chicken that are traditionally baked in a tandoor (an Indian clay oven) and then cooked in a rich, creamy tomato sauce.

Sushi

The California Roll is a type of sushi roll that is popular in Western-style sushi cuisine. It consists of sushi rice, nori (seaweed), and fillings such as imitation crab meat (or real crab), avocado, and cucumber.
